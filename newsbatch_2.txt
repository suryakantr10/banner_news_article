Title: Big Whiskey’s Expanding to Coral Springs
Status hint: Opening | Industry: Specialty / Other
Published: 2026-05-05 21:14:56

CORAL SPRINGS, FL — Steve Swanson, alongside his sister Jacki Swanson and his wife Suzanne Swanson, is bringing Big Whiskey’s American Restaurant & Bar to 3374 North University Drive, Coral Springs, FL 33065 . Swanson told Business Debut the team is targeting a September opening, with a possibility of opening in August if construction stays on track. This is the group’s second Big Whiskey’s location, following its existing restaurant in Davie. What to Expect Big Whiskey’s is a full-service, family-friendly restaurant and bar. Swanson described it as “a family friendly restaurant that celebrates sports. And we have whiskey." The Coral Springs location will feature about 180 indoor seats and 50 to 60 seats on a shaded patio. Inside, guests can expect 37 televisions and a layout designed for both dining and watching live sports, Swanson said. Images courtesy of Big Whiskey's American Restaurant & Bar, Coral Springs. Menu and Vision The menu will include burgers, wraps, wings, steaks, salads, pastas, seafood and sandwiches, Swanson told Business Debut. He said guests can expect cheeseburgers, craft burgers, chicken wings, bone-in and boneless wings, chicken sandwiches, turkey melts, Philly cheesesteaks and a range of barbecue. Swanson highlighted the bourbon glazed salmon, ribs, Big Whiskey barbecue burger, bourbon street burger and crunch wrap chicken as popular items. “We’ve got things that people love with a Big Whiskey twist,” Swanson told Business Debut.“We have really good steaks that people can come in and enjoy without it being over-the-top expensive.” The bar program will feature roughly 50 to 100 whiskeys at any given time, including popular labels and rotating selections. Swanson said Big Whiskey’s is not meant to feel like a dark, traditional whiskey bar. “Our environment is family friendly, high energy, great bar,” Swanson told Business Debut.

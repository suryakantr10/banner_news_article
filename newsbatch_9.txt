Ryan Kneller The property was previously home to the last Lehigh Valley location of Ruby Tuesday, a casual, full-service chain known for its "simple fresh American dining," which closed in January 2025 . The Bethlehem Township location will total approximately 4,300 square feet and will feature a new outdoor dining patio, Garrett said. The restaurant will offer seating for more than 170 guests across its indoor and outdoor dining areas, and there also will be a locally inspired mural, Garrett said. Hours will be 7 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. daily.

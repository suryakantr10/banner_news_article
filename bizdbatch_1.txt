Menu and Vision The brewery will focus on lagers, particularly European-style and German-style lagers, Myers told Business Debut. Myers said he is especially excited to serve bock beer, a darker German lager. “It’s a richer flavor than your domestic lagers,” Myers told Business Debut. “There’s a lot of different German beers that just haven’t gotten their spotlight in America.” Opening Timeline Three Rabbits Brewing is targeting a soft opening after its first beers are ready.

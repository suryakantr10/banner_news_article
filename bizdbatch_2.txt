He said the concept is meant to bring the same level of daily cafe experience found in cities like New York or Paris to St. Augustine. Opening Timeline Bensimon told Business Debut the cafe is targeting a full opening within about two weeks, pending final permit and health approvals. In the meantime, he said Maison Louis Cafe is already welcoming guests during its soft opening. Hours Maison Louis Cafe is currently operating from 7 a.m. to 4 p.m. seven days a week

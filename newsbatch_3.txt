Title: New art gallery Visual Feast now open in Hoover
Status hint: Opening | Industry: Specialty / Other
Published: 2026-05-06 16:34:08

Facebook LinkedIn Tweet New art gallery Visual Feast is located at 4421 Creekside Avenue, Suite 117. (Mary Helene Hall / Bham Now) A new art gallery in Patton Creek Shopping Center in Hoover is seeking up-and-coming artists to join its gallery community. Visual Feast Gallery is the manifestation of 78-year-old abstract artist Rose Cook’s lifelong dream. The gallery, which opened in February, features a curated selection of: Cook’s original abstract art Handmade jewelry Vintage finds Featured traditional artists View this post on Instagram A post shared by Rose Cook (@visualfeastgallery) The space, located at 4421 Creekside Avenue, Suite 117 , also offers: Art classes Creative workshops Gallery rentals for small events Rose Cook’s journey to Visual Feast Cook moved to Birmingham in 2025, but she has been professionally creating art since 1986, according to the gallery’s website . After beginning her career in sales and marketing, she decided to start her own company to sell handmade jewelry designed for comfort as well as style. What started as a one-woman show eventually grew to have four sales representatives and two people working in manufacturing. She began painting 15 years after she started her business. Cook’s work focuses on abstracts and floating images, and the result is creations with “interesting colors, a sense of movement and visual textures,” her website says. “I believe that art is an important expression of personal environment and style. It must be embraced emotionally, and it must meet the owner’s design objectives and personality. I want buyers who are enthusiastic about my limited edition, one-of-a-kind pieces.

The Tennessee-based brand started as a small operation in Franklin, Tenn., in 2020 before expanding into Nashville and Atlanta. Commercial real estate records list Carbon Performance as the tenant at 3325 S. Tryon St., with a lease for about 43,833 square feet, per Fowler Property Advisors . That footprint would make it one of the largest single‑site fitness projects to hit Lower South End in recent years. What You'll Find Inside The club’s Charlotte page outlines more than 220 pieces of equipment, including 175+ strength machines, 40+ cardio machines and 20+ cable stations, according to Carbon Performance . Plans also call for a 4,000‑square‑foot women’s‑only training area and a dedicated reformer Pilates studio. Programming will range from group classes and HYROX sessions to staffed personal training. The company also touts a macro‑friendly shake bar and an on-site retail shop stocked with supplements and apparel. Locker rooms are set to feature dry saunas and a 360‑degree posing room so members can track progress under flattering, photo-ready lighting.
